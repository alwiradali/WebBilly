Our Story

A family recipe,
a modern table.

Saffron House began with a grandmother's spice tin and a simple belief: food should bring people together. Three generations later, that's still the whole idea.

  • ✓Spices ground in-house every morning for depth you can taste.
  • ✓Locally sourced meat, veg and dairy from trusted suppliers.
  • ✓Vegan & gluten-free menus given the same love as everything else.
