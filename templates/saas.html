What Pulse Does

Everything you need to understand your product

One platform for tracking, analysis and action — from the first pageview to the power user.

📈

Real-time dashboards

Live metrics that update the moment an event fires. Build boards in minutes and share them with a link — no refresh, no waiting.

🔻

Funnel analysis

Map any multi-step flow and spot exactly where users drop off. Break it down by cohort, device or plan without touching a query.

🔔

Alerts & automation

Set thresholds on any metric and get pinged in Slack the second something moves. Trigger webhooks to keep the rest of your stack in sync.

🧭

Retention & cohorts

See who comes back and who slips away. Slice retention by signup week, feature adoption or acquisition channel in a single click.

🗺️

Session paths

Follow the real journeys users take through your product and surface the flows that convert — and the detours that don't.

🔒

Governance & access

SSO, role-based permissions and a managed event schema keep your data clean, private and audit-ready as your team scales.
