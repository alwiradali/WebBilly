Frequently asked questions

Will I lose my Google rankings in a redesign?

Not if it's done right. We carry over your SEO and set up redirects so your rankings are protected — and usually improved.

Can you keep my existing content?

Yes — we can keep, refresh or rewrite your content depending on what you want.

How long does a redesign take?

Most redesigns take one to three weeks depending on size and content readiness.

Do I need to change my hosting or domain?

No — you keep your domain, and we can either use your current hosting or move you to faster managed hosting.
