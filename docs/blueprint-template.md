# Billy Digitals — "Blueprint" reply template

Reusable reply for anyone who asks for "your blueprint / plan / proposal"
(WhatsApp or email). Keep it short — it delivers value AND filters for
serious buyers by ending with a price + a small commitment step.

**Before sending:** fill in `{{NAME}}` and `{{PRICE}}` (your real "from"
starter price, e.g. `KSh ____ / £____`). Vague pricing invites endless
back-and-forth; a real "from" figure qualifies fast.

---

> Hey {{NAME}}, here's the quick blueprint 👇
>
> 1️⃣ **Pick a lane** — offer a skill/service, sell a digital product or mini-course, or a local-services site.
> 2️⃣ **I build your site** — clean, mobile, professional, with an enquiry/booking form + WhatsApp. Live in days.
> 3️⃣ **Get you found** — basic SEO + a Google profile so customers find you.
> 4️⃣ **Grow** — add payments, a store or a course as you earn.
>
> Start lean from **{{PRICE}}** — clear fixed quote, no hidden fees.
>
> Reply with the lane that excites you (1, 2 or 3) and I'll send your exact quote + book you in. A small deposit secures your slot. 🚀
> 🌐 billydigitals.com
