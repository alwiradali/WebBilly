# Billy Digitals — Client-Getting Playbook

Ready-to-use copy for the parts of the Google plan that happen **off** your website.
Your site now handles the inbound half (SEO blog + the free checklist lead magnet at
`billydigitals.com/free-website-checklist.html`). This covers outreach and ads.

---

## 1. Google Search Ads (paste into Google Ads)

Point every ad at your **lead-magnet page** or your **contact section** — not the homepage.
Turn on conversion tracking using the `generate_lead` event you already have.

**Headlines (mix any 3, 30 chars max each):**
- Websites From Billy Digitals
- Small Business Web Design UK
- Get Found On Google Locally
- Free Website Review & Quote
- Fast, Affordable Websites
- Sites Built To Win Customers
- Free 27-Point Site Checklist

**Descriptions (90 chars max):**
- Professional, fast websites for UK small businesses. Free quote in 24 hours. No jargon.
- We build sites that get found and get customers. Grab our free website checklist today.

**Keywords to target (start with "phrase match"):**
- "web designer near me"
- "small business website"
- "website design [your town]"
- "affordable web design uk"
- "shopify website designer"

**Daily budget tip:** start at £5–£10/day, one campaign, and let it run 2 weeks before judging.

## 3. LinkedIn / cold outreach

Find local business owners (by town + industry). Keep it short, human, no hard sell.

**Connection note (300 chars):**
> Hi [Name] — I build websites for [industry] businesses around [town] and came across
> [Company]. Not pitching anything, just always happy to connect with local owners. 👋

**Follow-up message (after they accept):**
> Thanks for connecting, [Name]! Quick one — I put together a free 27-point checklist for
> small-business websites (the stuff that quietly wins or loses customers). Want me to send
> it over? No strings. Here it is either way: billydigitals.com/free-website-checklist.html

---

## 4. Cold email (keep it 4 sentences)

**Subject lines (test a few):**
- Quick idea for [Company]'s website
- Found [Company] — one thing worth a look
- Free website checklist for [Company]

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> I build fast, professional websites for [industry] businesses around [town] — recently
> [ATB Premier Travel / Heat On].
>
> I made a free 27-point checklist of what a small-business site needs to get found and win
> customers. Want me to send it over? Happy to do a quick free review of [website] too — no
> pressure at all.
>
> Cheers,
> Ali — Billy Digitals
> billydigitals.com · WhatsApp 07519 022117

**Rules:** personalise the first line every time, never attach files (link instead), and
always give them an easy out. Send in small batches so you can reply fast.

## 5. What to do this week
1. Share the checklist link on your WhatsApp status, Google Business Profile post, and socials.
2. Turn on one small Google Search Ads campaign pointing at the checklist page.
3. Send 5 personalised LinkedIn connects a day to local owners.
4. Reply to every download within a day with a friendly "did it help?" message.

Everything ties back to the same asset — the free checklist — so every channel feeds one funnel.
